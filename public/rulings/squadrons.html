Count Dooku }

Keywords:adept 2, cloak, rogue
Card Text

While a friendly squadron without Ai at range 1-2 is attacking a squadron, it may suffer 1 damage. If it does, it may reroll 1 die. If that squadron has screen, it may instead reroll up to 2 dice.

Timing
This Squadron's first ability applies during all points in a turn when it is unactivated.
A squadron is considered activated once you announce that you are activating them. Their slider is then toggled at the end of the activation to track that they have been activated. This means that during this Squadron's activation, it would lose Dodge 1 and gain an additional Adept 1 from the ability.

Clarifications

  • This Squadron's ability applies to itself, as it is at distance 1 of itself and the ability doesn't specify “other” friendly squadrons. [1]
  • As with the rule on keywords, if a squadron gains a stackable keyword such as Adept or Dodge from multiple sources, unless specified otherwise through text like “to a maximum of”, this squadron's ability will stack. [1]

Upgrade Interactions

  • If this Squadron is the target of Flechette Torpedoes and its activation slider is toggled to the activated side, then this Squadron's ability would switch from granting Dodge 1 to Adept 1. [1]
  • If this Squadron is selected by 7I-99 to perform a Counter attack, as the activation slider is toggled after the Counter attack is completed, this Squadron's effect would still grant Dodge 1, and not Adept 1 until after the activation slider is toggled to the activated side. [1]
  • If this Squadron is activated before the start of the Squadron Phase, all friendly squadrons without Ai at distance 1 will have Adept, and therefore may resolve the first effect of Hot Landing to gain Grit. If a friendly squadron without Ai were to lose this Squadron's effect, as an example by moving out of range or this Squadron being destroyed, then it does not have Adept to resolve Hot Landing's second effect. [1]

Squadron Interactions

  • If Hondo Ohnaka (Slave I) activates this Squadron using its ability, then this Squadron switches from granting Dodge 1 to Adept 1. [1]
  • When this Squadron defends against an attack from Dutch Vander, if it is unactivated before the attack, it has Dodge 1 and may resolve the Dodge keyword on dice from Dutch Vander's attack. If this Squadron were to suffer damage to resolve Dutch's ability, and it may perform a Counter attack, then it would have Adept for that Counter attack as Dutch's ability resolves during the Resolve Damage step, and Counter attacks happen after the Resolve Damage step. [1]

Obstacle Interactions

  • If an Exogorth deals damage to this Squadron and toggles its activation slider to the activated side, then this Squadron switches from granting Dodge 1 to Adept 1. [1]

Darth Maul }

Keywords:cloak, dodge 1, rogue
Card Text

If you are engaged with at least 2 enemy squadrons, gain Adept 1. For each additional squadron you are engaged with, increase your Adept value by 1.

Timing
This Squadron's ability applies whenever it is engaged with 2 or more enemy squadrons.

Clarifications

  • This Squadron's ability applies to all attacks initiated by this squadron. For example, if this Squadron is engaged with 2 or more Heavy squadrons and performs an anti-ship attack, the engagement condition is met to activate this Squadron's ability, and therefore it has the Adept and may resolve it for that attack. [1]
  • Additional effects that key off of the Adept keyword apply to this Squadron when it meets the condition of its ability. [1]
  • Additional instances of the Adept keyword will stack with this Squadron's ability. For example, if Count Dooku (Squadron) gives this Squadron Adept 1, and this squadron is already engaged with 2 squadrons, it will increase its Adept by 1 to Adept 2. [1]
  • As this Squadron's ability specifies that the squadron gains Adept when under the engagement condition, Counter attacks made when the engagement condition is met may resolve Adept. [1]

Upgrade Interactions

  • If this Squadron is activated through TI-99 and gains Counter, if it is engaged with at least 2 enemy squadrons it may resolve the Adept keyword for that Counter attack. [1]
  • As this squadron has Adept gained as a keyword when the engagement condition is met, both effects of the Hot Landing upgrade card apply to this squadron while the condition is active. In addition, if this squadron were to lose the engagement condition, therefore losing Adept, and it is destroyed, it will not resolve the second effect of Hot Landing as it no longer has that keyword. [1]

Squadron Interactions

  • Squadrons that ignore engagement, such as Anakin Skywalker (BTL-B), do not count towards the engaged squadron total while their ability is active, as they bypass keywords or card effects that trigger on engagement. For example, this Squadron is engaged with two squadrons, a generic non-Escort squadron and Anakin Skywalker (BTL-B). If Anakin Skywalker (BTL-B) activates, spends a defense token to ignore engagement, and then performs an attack against this Squadron, if this Squadron is to perform a Counter attack against Anakin Skywalker (BTL-B), then that Counter attack will not gain the benefit of this squadron's effect, as it is “only engaged” with one squadron for the duration of the ability. [1]
